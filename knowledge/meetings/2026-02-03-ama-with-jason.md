### SKU Rationalization Strategy

Two-part product strategy:

**Part 1 — Consolidation:**

- Reduce custom product categories from 14 → 5 (e.g., bakery & cafe, candy, snacks)
- Goal: increase quality and variety _within_ each category, create more linear customer flow
- Seasonal SKUs NOT being eliminated — important for freshness and conversation, but will be made more consistent year-over-year to improve demand prediction and reduce waste

**Part 2 — Branded Physical Products:**

- Standalone branded products (custom mugs) as proven revenue generator
- Quickly add: water bottles, totes, clothing — products clients perceive as "from me" not "a SugarWish"
- Kelly Kocher feedback: clients value new options year-over-year for variety; Jason acknowledged this tension and hopes new product lines compensate
